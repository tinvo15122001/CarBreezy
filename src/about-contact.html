About us

CarBreezy is a leading automotive digital marketplace that seeks to make car buying and selling easy,
transparent and efficient.

From discovery to delivery, consumers can use TrueCar to explore new and used vehicles from an expansive,
cross-brand selection of inventory from our vast network of
Certified Dealers.

Founded in 2022, Carbreezy is building a trusted brand and strong
reputation for providing consumers with useful tools, research,
market context, and price transparency. as they begin their car-buying
journey. The company is bringing even more to consumers' online
shopping process to find a new or used car that's right for them,
secure financing, sell or trade in their current vehicle, and complete
their purchase with a reputable dealer that they Home to.
